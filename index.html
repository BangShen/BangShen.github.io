About Me

Hi, my name is ShenBang
This is my first personal blog built on August 2018 with the purpose of sharing my data analysis related projects and other life perceptions, furthermore, I also want to record my life via this online way and let more people know me.

I am 25 years old this year, and I am just graduated and finished my master degree at Shanghai Jiaotong University. Now I am currently a researcher in a lithium-related production company in China.
I am interested in using my background and experience in the field of data analysis, as far as I know, data analysis has been applied in every field such as IT, insurance, financial even in a manufacturing factory, a different field has different requirements when it comes to data analysis skills. And for me, I prefer to do data analysis of financial data since the financial company possesses lots of users’ data, insights can be found by analyzing those transaction data, that will be fantastic.
